About the clinic

Personal, evidence-based aesthetic care

Rahfan Aesthetic was founded to bring a more transparent, patient-first approach to cosmetic dermatology in Lahore. Every plan begins with an honest consultation — no upselling, just a clear path to the result you want.

The clinic is led personally on every visit, with treatments backed by current dermatological evidence and FDA-approved devices. We keep our patient list intentionally manageable so every appointment gets full attention.
